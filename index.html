4. Best Online Jobs for Students in the Philippines

Many companies now hire remote workers, making it easier for students to earn money online while studying. Students can work as virtual assistants, freelance writers, social media managers, graphic designers, or online sellers. These jobs are flexible and allow students to manage their own time.

5. How to Start Affiliate Marketing for Beginners

Affiliate marketing allows you to earn commissions by promoting products online. You can share affiliate links on your website or social media. When someone buys using your link, you earn a percentage.
